BioTuring 生態系總覽

認識四大無程式碼平台，理解 BioTuring 在單細胞與空間分析中的定位與取捨。

Meet the four no-code platforms and understand where BioTuring fits in single-cell & spatial analysis.

概覽

BioTuring 是什麼？為誰而做？

想像你剛買了一台高階洗衣機。裡面其實藏著複雜的馬達控制、水溫演算法、脫水轉速曲線——但你面對的只是一片面板：「快洗」「羽絨」「除菌」，按一下就好。你不需要懂電機工程，衣服照樣洗得乾淨。BioTuring 做的，就是把繁複的單細胞分析變成像操作智慧家電面板：底層是一整套嚴謹的統計與機器學習演算法，但你看到的是一顆顆清楚的按鈕。

BioTuring, Inc.（總部位於美國加州 San Diego）打造一系列免寫程式的圖形化分析平台（no-code GUI），讓沒有 R / Python 經驗的實驗室科學家，也能獨立完成單細胞與空間體學分析。它的核心理念很單純：把標準的 scRNA-seq 與空間分析流程「按鈕化」——你在介面上點選，軟體就在背後跑對應的演算法，並即時把結果畫成圖給你看。

這家公司不算小眾玩具：官網宣稱服務超過 1 萬名科學家、90+ 家企業、300+ 研究機構、遍及 20 個國家，並通過 ISO/IEC 27001（資訊安全）與 27701（隱私）認證——對處理病人檢體的臨床實驗室來說，這是重要的信任門檻。

因此本教程有兩個目標：① 教你在 BioTuring 平台上「怎麼點」；② 對照每顆按鈕背後的演算法概念（例如 UMAP、Louvain、Harmony、Venice），讓你不只是操作，而是理解。畢竟，會按「快洗」很好，但知道「羽絨衣為什麼不能高速脫水」，才不會把資料洗壞。

Picture a high-end washing machine you just bought. Inside hides complex motor control, water-temperature algorithms, spin-speed curves — but all you face is a panel: "Quick Wash," "Down," "Sanitize." One press and done. You don't need an electrical-engineering degree; your clothes still come out clean. BioTuring does exactly this for single-cell analysis: it turns a famously fiddly pipeline into something that feels like a smart-appliance panel. Underneath sits a rigorous stack of statistics and machine learning; what you see are clearly labeled buttons.

BioTuring, Inc. (headquartered in San Diego, California) builds a family of no-code graphical (GUI) analysis platforms, so wet-lab scientists without R/Python experience can run single-cell and spatial omics analysis on their own. The core idea is simple: turn the standard scRNA-seq / spatial pipeline into buttons — you click in the interface, the software runs the matching algorithm behind the scenes, and it draws the results for you in real time.

This is not a niche toy: the company says it serves over 10,000 scientists, 90+ companies, 300+ research institutions across 20 countries, and is certified to ISO/IEC 27001 (information security) and 27701 (privacy) — an important trust bar for clinical labs handling patient specimens.

So this tutorial has two goals: (1) teach you which buttons to click; and (2) map each button to the algorithm concept behind it (UMAP, Louvain, Harmony, Venice…), so you understand rather than just operate. Knowing how to hit "Quick Wash" is great — but knowing why you shouldn't spin a down jacket at max speed is what keeps you from ruining the load.

💡
學習定位：如果你已學過 Seurat / Scanpy，本教程幫你把觀念對應到 GUI；如果你是完全新手，BioTuring 是「先看見結果、再回頭理解原理」的友善入口。兩種讀者都建議搭配我們的 scRNA-seq 互動教學 對照演算法細節。 How to use this: If you already know Seurat/Scanpy, this maps concepts onto the GUI; if you're new, BioTuring is a friendly "see results first, understand later" entry point. Either way, pair it with our scRNA-seq interactive tutorial for algorithm depth.
🎯
為什麼這件事重要：單細胞資料動輒上萬個細胞、兩三萬個基因，是一個「幾億格數字」的巨型表格。手寫程式跑完一輪 QC→標準化→分群→註釋，新手常要卡上好幾天、還可能因為一個參數打錯而全盤重來。GUI 平台把這段學習曲線壓成一個下午——代價是彈性變少（後面第四節細談）。理解這個交換，你才知道什麼時候該把工具切換回程式碼。 Why this matters: A single-cell dataset routinely has tens of thousands of cells × ~20,000 genes — a giant table of hundreds of millions of numbers. Hand-coding one full pass of QC → normalization → clustering → annotation can trap a newcomer for days, and a single mistyped parameter can force a total redo. A GUI platform compresses that learning curve into an afternoon — at the cost of flexibility (detailed in Section IV). Understanding this trade is what tells you when to switch back to code.
四大平台

一、生態系的四位「團隊成員」

與其把 BioTuring 想成「四個軟體」，不如把它想成一支四人小隊——每位成員個性鮮明、各司其職，但常常需要接力合作。認識他們的分工，你就不會拿著空間資料去敲 bulk 分析師的門。

Rather than "four apps," think of BioTuring as a four-person team — each with a distinct personality and job, but often passing work down the line. Know who does what, and you won't hand spatial data to the bulk analyst by mistake.

🔬

BBrowserX / BBrowserX Pro

隊長，主力打者。旗艦單細胞 RNA-seq 分析平台，也是本教程從 Step 1 到多模態的主場。個性：又快又猛——GPU 加速，Pro 版可擴展到 1,000 萬+ 細胞。從匯入、QC、標準化、分群、細胞類型自動註釋，到差異表達（內建 Venice）、軌跡分析（Monocle 2）、細胞通訊（CellChat），一條龍全包。支援 scRNA/snRNA、TCR/BCR、CITE-seq (ADT) 與樣本 demultiplexing。可讀 .h5ad、Seurat 物件、10x HDF5、MTX。

The captain, the cleanup hitter. The flagship single-cell RNA-seq platform, and the home base for this tutorial from Step 1 through multimodal. Personality: fast and forceful — GPU-accelerated, Pro scales to 10M+ cells. From import, QC, normalization, clustering and auto cell-type annotation, to differential expression (built-in Venice), trajectory (Monocle 2) and cell communication (CellChat) — the whole pipeline in one place. Supports scRNA/snRNA, TCR/BCR, CITE-seq (ADT), and demultiplexing. Reads .h5ad, Seurat objects, 10x HDF5, MTX.

🗺️

SpatialX (前身 Lens)

製圖師，重視「在哪裡」。空間生物學分析平台（Windows、無程式碼）。BBrowserX 告訴你「有哪些細胞」，SpatialX 告訴你「這些細胞排在組織的哪個位置、跟誰當鄰居」。支援 Visium、Xenium、MERFISH、CosMx、GeoMx、Slide-seq，能做鄰近細胞分析與細胞間交互作用。史丹佛與東京大學的團隊都是使用者。見 SpatialX 章節。

The cartographer — obsessed with "where." The spatial biology platform (Windows, no-code). BBrowserX tells you which cells you have; SpatialX tells you where they sit in the tissue and who their neighbors are. Supports Visium, Xenium, MERFISH, CosMx, GeoMx, Slide-seq, with neighborhood and cell–cell interaction analysis. Teams at Stanford and the University of Tokyo are users. See the SpatialX chapter.

🔎

Talk2Data

圖書館員兼偵探。公開資料的分析中樞與資料庫：1.2 億+ 精選單細胞、跨研究分析、Gene Search、自訂細胞圖譜。你甚至不用自己做實驗：想知道「某個基因在哪些細胞型別表現最高」，直接在整個資料庫裡查。BBrowserX 的自動註釋也正是靠這座圖書館撐腰。見 Talk2Data 章節。

Librarian meets detective. An analytics hub & database of public data: 120M+ curated cells, cross-study analysis, Gene Search, custom atlases. You don't even need your own experiment: want to know which cell types express a gene most highly? Query the whole database. It's also the library that powers BBrowserX's auto-annotation. See the Talk2Data chapter.

📊

SmartBulk

資深老將，做的是「傳統活」。Bulk RNA-seq 分析平台，補足生態系的整體轉錄組分析（差異表達、通路富集等）。Bulk 就像把整鍋湯打成一杯——量到的是所有細胞的平均訊號，看不到單一細胞的差異，但便宜、成熟、樣本量大時仍非常實用。本教程僅概覽，不深入。

The seasoned veteran doing the "classic job." The bulk RNA-seq platform, rounding out the ecosystem with whole-transcriptome analysis (DE, pathway enrichment). Bulk is like blending the whole soup into one cup — you measure the average signal across all cells, losing single-cell resolution, but it's cheap, mature, and still powerful at large sample sizes. Covered here only at overview level.

🧭
一句話記住分工：「有哪些細胞」問 BBrowserX；「細胞在哪裡」問 SpatialX；「別人已經知道什麼」問 Talk2Data；「整體平均長怎樣」問 SmartBulk。四個問題，四位隊友。 One line to remember them: "Which cells?" → BBrowserX. "Where are they?" → SpatialX. "What does the field already know?" → Talk2Data. "What's the bulk average?" → SmartBulk. Four questions, four teammates.
🧩
幕後功臣：你在按鈕上看不到、但一直在運作的還有 Signac（BioTuring 自研的單細胞分析套件，Venice 差異表達演算法就內建其中）、BioVinci（繪圖與視覺化）與 BioColab。它們不是給你直接操作的主角，而是讓四位隊友跑得更順的引擎室。 Behind the scenes: Invisible on the button panel but always running are Signac (BioTuring's single-cell toolkit — the Venice DE algorithm lives here), BioVinci (figure-making & visualization), and BioColab. Not front-line tools you click, but the engine room that keeps the four teammates humming.
分析旅程

二、一趟典型分析的完整旅程

光看四張名片還不夠——來看一趟真實的接力賽。假設你是免疫學實驗室的研究生，剛從定序公司拿回一批腫瘤浸潤淋巴球的 scRNA-seq 資料（約 4 萬個細胞）。你會這樣一站一站走過去：

Business cards aren't enough — let's watch a real relay race. Say you're a grad student in an immunology lab who just got back scRNA-seq data on tumor-infiltrating lymphocytes (~40,000 cells) from the sequencing core. Here's how you'd move station to station:

匯入與 QC（BBrowserX）

把 10x 的輸出（.h5 或 MTX）拖進 BBrowserX。第一件事是「品管」：踢掉空液滴、死細胞（高粒線體比例）與雙胞（doublet）。就像進場前的安檢，寧可嚴一點。4 萬 → 約 3.4 萬個乾淨細胞。

Drag the 10x output (.h5 or MTX) into BBrowserX. First job: quality control — drop empty droplets, dying cells (high mitochondrial %), and doublets. Like security at the door: better a little strict. 40k → ~34k clean cells.

標準化與批次校正（BBrowserX）

不同細胞抓到的分子數不一（定序深度差異），要先「拉齊音量」（log 標準化）。若你有多個病人樣本混在一起，用 Harmony 把「病人間的技術差異」壓下去，留住「真正的生物差異」。

Different cells capture different molecule counts (sequencing depth varies), so first "level the volume" (log normalization). If several patient samples are mixed, use Harmony to suppress batch (patient-level technical) differences while keeping the real biology.

降維、分群、視覺化（BBrowserX）

PCA 抓主要變異，UMAP 把兩萬維的細胞攤成一張可看的地圖，Louvain 把靠在一起的細胞圈成一群一群。螢幕上第一次出現那張「彩色島嶼圖」——通常是最有成就感的一刻。

PCA captures the main variation, UMAP flattens ~20,000-dimensional cells onto a readable map, and Louvain lassoes nearby cells into groups. The first time that "colorful archipelago" appears on screen — usually the most satisfying moment.

細胞類型註釋（BBrowserX + Talk2Data 支援）

每座島是什麼細胞？按下「自動預測」，BBrowserX 對照建於 1 億+ 單細胞 的參考庫，能區分 54 種細胞型別、183 種亞型。CD8 T、調節型 T、耗竭 T…名字一一貼上。這一步正是 Talk2Data 這座圖書館在幕後撐腰。

What is each island? Hit "auto-predict": BBrowserX matches against a reference built on 100M+ single cells, distinguishing 54 cell types and 183 subtypes. CD8 T, regulatory T, exhausted T… labels get stamped on. This step is exactly where the Talk2Data library backs you up.

差異表達與生物意義（Venice）

耗竭 T 細胞比一般 T 多表現了哪些基因？用 Venice 找 marker gene（它比傳統只比平均值的方法更懂「混合群體」），再做基因集富集（GSEA）、軌跡分析看 T 細胞如何一步步走向耗竭。故事，就從這裡長出來。

Which genes are up in exhausted vs. normal T cells? Use Venice to find markers (it handles "mixed populations" better than mean-only methods), then GSEA and trajectory to see how T cells slide toward exhaustion step by step. This is where the story grows.

出圖、報告、交棒

把 UMAP、violin plot、heatmap 匯出成投稿等級的圖；存成專案檔留紀錄。若接下來要做空間定位驗證，資料與註釋可以往 SpatialX 交棒——接力賽的下一棒就此展開。

Export UMAP, violin plots, heatmaps as publication-grade figures; save a project file for the record. If spatial validation comes next, hand the data and annotations to SpatialX — the relay's next leg begins.

🕵️
常見誤區：新手最容易「跳過 QC 直接看 UMAP」——因為 UMAP 圖太漂亮、太誘人。但垃圾進、垃圾出：沒清乾淨的死細胞和雙胞，會在後面偽裝成「一群神秘的新細胞型別」，害你追一個根本不存在的生物學故事。再急，也先做安檢。 Common trap: Beginners love to "skip QC and jump to UMAP" — the plot is just too pretty. But garbage in, garbage out: uncleaned dead cells and doublets masquerade as a "mysterious new cell type" downstream, sending you chasing biology that doesn't exist. However rushed — run security first.
資料規模

三、Talk2Data 精選資料庫的規模

還記得第 4 站的「自動註釋」為什麼那麼準嗎？因為它站在一座巨人般的圖書館上。BioTuring 的自動註釋與跨研究分析，建立在一個龐大的精選 (curated) 公開資料庫之上——關鍵字是「精選」：不是把 GEO 上的東西一股腦倒進來，而是經過統一清洗、註釋與品管的資料。下圖為官方公佈的各資料型態細胞／切片數量。

注意 Y 軸是對數尺度：這代表各型態之間的差距是「數量級」等級的天壤之別。scRNA-seq 的 1.2 億顆細胞，和 bulk 空間切片的 1,800 片，如果畫成一般線性長條，後者會小到你根本看不見——用對數軸才公平。

Remember why Station 4's auto-annotation was so accurate? Because it stands on a giant's library. BioTuring's auto-annotation and cross-study analysis rest on a large curated public database — and "curated" is the key word: it's not GEO dumped in wholesale, but data uniformly cleaned, annotated, and QC'd. 🧠
專家小撇步：「圖譜大 ≠ 一定適用你的樣本」。這座資料庫以人類與常見組織為主，若你研究的是罕見物種、特殊處理條件或全新的細胞狀態，自動註釋可能給你「最像的鄰居」而非「正確答案」。永遠用你自己的 marker gene 交叉驗證註釋結果——把 AI 當助手，不是法官。 Pro tip: "Big atlas ≠ always fits your sample." This database centers on human and common tissues. For rare species, unusual treatments, or brand-new cell states, auto-annotation may hand you the "nearest neighbor," not the "right answer." Always cross-check labels with your own marker genes — treat the AI as an assistant, not a judge.

取捨

五、No-code GUI vs. 程式碼工具

這是每個做單細胞的人遲早要面對的抉擇，值得講清楚。打個比方：GUI 平台像自動排檔的車——上手快、通勤順、不用管離合器；Seurat / Scanpy 像手排跑車——換檔全靠你，累一點，但在賽道上你能榨出每一分性能、做出自排做不到的操作。兩者沒有誰「比較高級」，只有「這趟路適合開哪台」。

Every single-cell analyst faces this fork sooner or later, so let's be clear. Analogy: a GUI platform is an automatic-transmission car — quick to learn, smooth for commuting, no clutch to worry about. Seurat / Scanpy is a manual sports car — you shift everything yourself, more work, but on the track you can wring out every drop of performance and do things an automatic can't. Neither is "classier"; it's about which car suits this trip.

🗺️ 什麼時候該用哪個？（情境對照）

A→「我想在一週內看到初步結果、向 PI 報告方向」：用 BioTuring。快速探索、註釋、出圖，先抓到故事線。"I need preliminary results this week to brief my PI on direction": use BioTuring. Explore, annotate, make figures fast — grab the storyline first.
B→「我要投稿高分期刊，審稿人會要求完整可重現流程」：主分析建議 Seurat / Scanpy，每一步都能寫進 methods、附上腳本。"I'm submitting to a top journal; reviewers will demand a fully reproducible pipeline": run the main analysis in Seurat / Scanpy — every step goes into Methods with scripts attached.
C→「我需要一個標準流程做不到的客製方法（例如新的整合演算法）」：只能 程式碼。GUI 給你的是精選好菜單，不是無限自助餐。"I need a custom method the standard pipeline can't do (e.g., a new integration algorithm)": code only. A GUI gives you a curated menu, not an infinite buffet.
D→「我不寫程式，但想快速確認某基因在公開資料裡的表現」：Talk2Data 幾分鐘搞定，程式碼路線要花好幾天下載整合。"I don't code, but want to quickly check a gene across public data": Talk2Data in minutes; the code route would take days of downloading & integrating.
⚠️
不是二選一。最成熟的實務往往是「兩台車都開」：用 BioTuring 快速探索、註釋、做圖、抓方向，把最有希望的線索再用 Seurat / Scanpy 做客製化、可重現的正式分析與投稿版本。它們互補，不是取代——會用 GUI 的人，如果也懂一點程式碼，戰力最強。 Not either/or. The most mature practice is "drive both cars": explore, annotate, plot, and find direction fast in BioTuring, then take the most promising leads into Seurat / Scanpy for the customized, reproducible, submission-grade analysis. They complement, not replace — the strongest analyst uses the GUI and also reads a little code.

六、這門教程怎麼讀？你會學到什麼？

你現在站在起點。這門教程的骨架，正是第二節那趟「分析旅程」——每一站各有一章，先教你在 BBrowserX 上怎麼點，再帶你看懂按鈕背後的演算法。建議照順序讀，但每章也能單獨查閱。

You're at the starting line. This tutorial's spine is exactly the "analysis journey" from Section II — each station gets a chapter, teaching you which buttons to click in BBrowserX and then unpacking the algorithm behind them. Best read in order, but each chapter also stands alone as a reference.

📥

核心流程 (Step 1–6)

匯入 → QC → 標準化 → 分群 → 註釋 → 差異表達。scRNA-seq 分析的「主餐」，從 資料匯入 開始一路走到 差異表達。

Import → QC → Normalize → Cluster → Annotate → DE. The "main course" of scRNA-seq analysis, from Import all the way to DE.

🧬

進階與多模態

軌跡分析（細胞如何隨時間演變）與 多模態／免疫圖譜（TCR/BCR、CITE-seq）。當「有哪些細胞」不夠，你想問「它們怎麼變、免疫如何回應」。

Trajectory (how cells evolve over time) and Multimodal / immune (TCR/BCR, CITE-seq). For when "which cells" isn't enough and you ask "how they change, how immunity responds."

🗺️

空間與資料庫

SpatialX（細胞在組織的位置）與 Talk2Data（挖掘公開資料）。旅程的延伸棒次，把單細胞放回空間與已知知識的脈絡。

SpatialX (cells in tissue space) and Talk2Data (mining public data). The journey's later legs — putting single cells back into spatial and known-knowledge context.

✅
讀完你會帶走什麼：①在 BBrowserX 獨立跑完一套 scRNA-seq 分析的能力；②看懂 UMAP、Louvain、Harmony、Venice 等關鍵詞在「做什麼、為什麼」；③判斷何時該切換到 Seurat/Scanpy 的判斷力；④用 Talk2Data 與 SpatialX 延伸問題的視野。每章末尾都有 自我檢測，答錯不扣分，只幫你確認觀念有沒有卡住。 What you'll walk away with: (1) the ability to run a full scRNA-seq analysis in BBrowserX on your own; (2) understanding of what UMAP, Louvain, Harmony, Venice actually do and why; (3) the judgment to know when to switch to Seurat/Scanpy; (4) the vision to extend questions with Talk2Data and SpatialX. Every chapter ends with a self-check — no penalties, just a way to catch stuck concepts.
